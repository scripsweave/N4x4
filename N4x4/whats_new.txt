What's New in N4x4

AirPods Pro 3 Heart Rate
- Wear AirPods Pro 3 and see your live heart rate during workouts — no watch or strap needed (iOS 26 required). Enable it under Settings → Heart Rate Sources.
- Using more than one sensor? Choose which wins: reorder Bluetooth monitor, Apple Watch, and AirPods in Settings.
- Powerbeats Pro 2 also work — pair them as a Bluetooth heart rate monitor.

Redesigned Settings
- Settings is now organised like the iOS Settings app: grouped, searchable, and two taps to anything.
- Your most-used settings — default workout and training days — sit right at the top and show their current value at a glance.
- Reminders are now individually controllable: night-before, morning-of, and comeback nudges.

Choose Your Default Workout
- Pick the workout you actually do — Cycle, Run, Kettlebells, and more — and it's pre-selected every time you log a session.
- Kettlebells is now a fully supported exercise, with setup guidance and per-interval rep logging.

Better Interval Haptics
- A haptic countdown before every interval change: two short taps, then a long buzz as the new interval starts — on both iPhone and Apple Watch, whatever your audio setting.

Plus
- Training Tips now lives on the Home screen.
- The post-workout summary shows the workout type right at the top.
- Fixed: sessions tracked with the Apple Watch app no longer log a duplicate workout in Apple Health — every session is exactly one workout.
